How to Edit Offices

1. To edit Office, click the Table Row as shown in Figure 7-1. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for updating the existing Office (See Figure 7-4).


Figure 7-4 Office Scheme Form


2. Select the Parent Office on the dropdown list.

3. Type in the Name of Office, Alias (short name), Function, First Name, M.I., Surname, and Suffix on the entry boxes provided.

4. When done, click the Save button to update the existing Office or the Close (x) icon button to close the form.
