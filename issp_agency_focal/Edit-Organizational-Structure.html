How to Edit Organizational Structure

1. To edit Organizational Structure, click the Strategic Plan button as shown in Figure 5. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for updating the exisitng Organizational Structure (See Figure 5-3).


Figure 5-3 Organizational Structure Scheme Form


2. Type in the Number of Employees of the Filled-up Plantilla Positions Male and Female, Contractual Male and Female, and Non-Permanent Male and Female on the entry boxes provided.

3. Select the Source of Funds on the dropdown list.

4. Type in the Amount on the entry box provided.

5. Click the Plus (+) icon button to add Current Annual ICT Budget. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding Current Annual ICT Budget.

6. Type in the Present ICT Situation (Strategic Challenges) on the entry box provided.

7. Click the Add New button to add Strategic Concerns for ICT Use. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding Strategic Concerns for ICT Use (See Figure 5-4).

8. When done, click the Save button to update the existing Organizational Structure.
