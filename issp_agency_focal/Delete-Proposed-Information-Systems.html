How to Delete Proposed Information Systems

1. To delete Proposed Information Systems, click the Table Row on the Proposed Information Systems (See Figure 5-10). Click the Delete button (See Figure 5-13).


Figure 5-13 Delete Proposed Information Systems


2. Once you click the Delete button, delete dialog box will pop-up (See Figure 5-14).


Figure 5-14 Delete Dialog Box


3. Click the Delete button to proceed or click the Cancel button to do otherwise. A confirmation message will be displayed if the action is successful.
