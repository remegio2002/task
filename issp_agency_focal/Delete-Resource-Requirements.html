How to Delete Resource Requirements

1. To delete Resource Requirements, click the Table Row on the ICT Items as shown in Figure 5-25. Click the Delete button (See Figure 5-28).


Figure 5-28 Delete Resource Requirements


2. Once you click the Delete button, delete dialog box will pop-up (See Figure 5-29).


Figure 5-29 Delete Dialog Box


3. Click the Delete button to proceed or click the Cancel button to do otherwise. A confirmation message will be displayed if the action is successful.
