How to Add Accounts

1. To add Account, click the Add New button as shown in Figure 7-8. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding new Account (See Figure 7-9).


Figure 7-9 User Account Scheme Form


2. Type in the First Name, M.I., Surname, Suffix, Email Address, Contact Number, and Position on the entry boxes provided.

3. Select the Office on the dropdown list.

4. Click the Plus (+) icon button to add Office. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding new Office (See Figure 7-3).

5. When done, click the Save button to add new Account or the Cancel button to close the form.
