How to Edit Strategic Concerns for ICT Use

1. To edit Strategic Concerns for ICT Use, click the Table Row on the Strategic Concerns for ICT Use (See Figure 5-3). Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for updating the existing Strategic Concerns for ICT Use (See Figure 5-5).


Figure 5-5 Strategic Concerns for ICT Use Scheme Form


2. Type in and select the Major Final Output (MFO) / Organizational Outcome on the dropdown list.

3. Type in the Critical Management / Operating / Business Systems, Problems, and Intended Use of ICT on the entry boxes provided.

4. Click the Plus (+) icon button to add new entry boxes for Problems and Intended Use of ICT.

5. When done, click the Save button to update the existing Strategic Concerns for ICT Use or the Close (x) icon button to close the form.
