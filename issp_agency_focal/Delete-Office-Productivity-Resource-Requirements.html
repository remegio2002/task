How to Delete Office Productivity Resource Requirements

1. To delete Office Productivity Resource Requirements, click the Table Row on the ICT Items as shown in Figure 5-51. Click the Delete button (See Figure 5-54).


Figure 5-54 Delete Office Productivity Resource Requirements


2. Once you click the Delete button, delete dialog box will pop-up (See Figure 5-55).


Figure 5-55 Delete Dialog Box


3. Click the Delete button to proceed or click the Cancel button to do otherwise. A confirmation message will be displayed if the action is successful.
