How to Edit Cross Agency ICT Projects

1. To edit Cross Agency ICT Projects, click the Table Row as shown in Figure 5-40. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for updating the existing Cross Agency ICT Projects (See Figure 5-42).


Figure 5-42 Cross Agency ICT Projects Scheme Form


2. Type in the Name / Title of ICT Project on the entry box provided.

3. Select the From and To for the Duration on the datepicker.

4. Type in the Objectives and Deliverables on the entry boxes provided.

5. Select the Lead Agency and Implementing Agencies on the dropdown lists.

6. When done, click the Save button to update the existing Cross Agency ICT Projects or the Close (x) icon button to close the form.
