How to Add Offices

1. To add Office, click the Add New button as shown in Figure 7-2. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding new Office (See Figure 7-3).


Figure 7-3 Office Scheme Form


2. Select the Parent Office on the dropdown list.

3. Type in the Name of Office, Alias (short name), First Name, M.I., Surname, and Suffix on the entry boxes provided.

4. When done, click the Save button to add new Office or the Cancel button to cancel the operation.
