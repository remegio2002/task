How to Add Cross Agency ICT Projects

1. To add Cross Agency ICT Projects, click the Add New button (See Figure 5-40). Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding new Cross Agency ICT Projects (See Figure 5-41).


Figure 5-41 Cross Agency ICT Projects Scheme Form


2. Type in the Name / Title of ICT Project on the entry box provided.

3. Select the From and To for the Duration on the datepicker.

4. Type in the Objectives and Deliverables on the entry boxes provided.

5. Select the Lead Agency and Implementing Agencies on the dropdown lists.

6. When done, click the Save button to add new Cross Agency ICT Projects or the Cancel button to close the form.
