How to Add Strategic Concerns for ICT Use

1. To add Strategic Concerns for ICT Use, click the Add New button (See Figure 5-3). Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding new Strategic Concerns for ICT Use (See Figure 5-4).


Figure 5-4 Strategic Concerns for ICT Use Scheme Form


2. Type in and select the Major Final Output (MFO) / Organizational Outcome on the dropdown list.

3. Type in the Critical Management / Operating / Business Systems, Problems, and Intended Use of ICT on the entry boxes provided.

4. Click the Plus (+) icon button to add Problems and Intended Use of ICT. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding Problems and Intended Use of ICT.

When done, click the Save button to add new Strategic Concerns for ICT Use or the Cancel button to close the form.
