How to Preview ISSP Report

1. To preview the ISSP Report, click the Report tab (See Figure 6). Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for generating the Information Systems Strategic Plan Report (See Figure 6-1).


Figure 6-1 Preview ISSP Report Scheme Form


2. Type in the Title Name on the entry box provided.

3. Select the Year and Section on the dropdown lists.

4. When done, click the Preview button to generate the Information Systems Strategic Plan Report.
