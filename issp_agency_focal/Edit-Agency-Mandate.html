How to Edit Agency Mandate

1. To edit Agency Mandate, click the Mandate button as shown in Figure 4-6. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for updating the exisitng Agency Mandate (See Figure 4-6).


Figure 4-6 Agency Mandate Scheme Form


2. Type in the Legal Basis, Functions, Vision Statement, and Mission Statement on the entry boxes provided.

3. To upload the Agency and Its Environment (Functional Interface Chart), click the Browse button. A File Upload window will appear. Locate the image file to be uploaded, then click the Open button.

4. When done, click the Save button to update the existing Agency Mandate.
