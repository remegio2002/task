How to Add Organizational Chart

1. To add Organizational Chart, click the Agency Profile button (See Figure 4). Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding new Organizational Chart (See Figure 4-2).


Figure 4-2 Organizational Chart Scheme Form


2. To upload the Organizational Chart, click the Browse button. A File Upload window will appear. Locate the image file to be uploaded, then click the Open button.

3. Type in the No. of Regional/Extension Offices, No. of Provincial Offices, No. of Other Offices, and No. of Actual Plantilla Positions on the entry boxes provided.

4. When done, click the Save button to add new Organizational Chart.
