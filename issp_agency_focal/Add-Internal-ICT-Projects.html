How to Add Internal ICT Projects

1. To add Internal ICT Projects, click the Add New button (See Figure 5-19). Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding new Internal ICT Projects (See Figure 5-20).


Figure 5-20 Internal ICT Projects Scheme Form


2. Type in the Name / Title of ICT Project on the entry box provided.

3. Select the From and To for the Duration on the datepicker.

4. Type in the Objectives and Deliverables on the entry boxes provided.

5. When done, click the Save button to add new Internal ICT Projects or the Cancel button to close the form.
