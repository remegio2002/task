How to Edit ICT Organizational Structure

1. To edit ICT Organizational Structure, click the Resource Requirements button as shown in Figure 5-50. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for updating the existing ICT Organizational Structure (See Figure 5-62).


Figure 5-62 ICT Organizational Structure Scheme Form


2. To upload the Existing ICT Organizational Structure, click the Browse button. A File Upload window will appear. Locate the image file to be uploaded, then click the Open button.

3. Select the Permanent Positions on the dropdown list.

4. Type in the Salary Grade and No. of Personnel on the entry boxes provided.

5. Click the Plus (+) icon button to add new entry boxes for Permanent Positions, Salary Grade, and No. of Personnel.

6. Select the Contractual Positions on the dropdown list.

7. Type in the Salary Grade and No. of Personnel on the entry boxes provided.

8. Click the Plus (+) icon button to add new entry boxes for Contractual Positions, Salary Grade, and No. of Personnel.

9. To upload the Proposed ICT Organizational Structure, click the Browse button. A File Upload window will appear. Locate the image file to be uploaded, then click the Open button.

10. Select the Permanent Positions on the dropdown list.

11. Type in the Salary Grade and No. of Personnel on the entry boxes provided.

12. Click the Plus (+) icon button to add new entry boxes for Permanent Positions, Salary Grade, and No. of Personnel.

13. Select the Contractual Positions on the dropdown list.

14. Type in the Salary Grade and No. of Personnel on the entry boxes provided.

15. Click the Plus (+) icon button to add new entry boxes for Contractual Positions, Salary Grade, and No. of Personnel.

16. To upload the Placement of the Proposed ICT Organizational Structure in the Agency Organizational Structure, click the Browse button. A File Upload window will appear. Locate the image file to be uploaded, then click the Open button.

17. When done, click the Save button to update the existing ICT Organizational Structure.
