How to Edit Accounts

1. To edit Account, click the Table Row as shown in Figure 7-7. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for updating the existing Account (See Figure 7-10).


Figure 7-10 Usser Account Scheme Form


2. Type in the First Name, M.I., Surname, Suffix, Email Address, Contact Number, and Position on the entry boxes provided.

3. Select the Office on the dropdown list.

4. Click the Plus (+) icon button to add Office. Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding new Office (See Figure 7-3).

5. When done, click the Save button to update the existing Account or the Close (x) icon button to close the form.
