How to Update Organizational Chart

1. To update Organizational Chart, fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for updating Organizational Chart (See Figure 4-2).


Figure 4-2 Organizational Chart Scheme Form


2. To upload the image file, click Browse button. A File Upload window will appear. Locate the image file to be uploaded, then click Open button.

3. Type in the No. of Regional/Extension Offices, No. of Provincial Offices, No. of Other Offices, and No. of Actual Plantilla Positions on the entry boxes provided.

4. When done, click the Save button to update the Organizational Chart.
