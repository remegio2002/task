How to Delete Grants-In-Aid (GIA) Projects

1. To delete Grants-In-Aid (GIA) Projects, click the Table Row as shown in Figure 5-35. Click the Delete button (See Figure 5-38).


Figure 5-38 Delete Grants-In-Aid (GIA) Projects


2. Once you click the Delete button, delete dialog box will pop-up (See Figure 5-39).


Figure 5-39 Delete Dialog Box


3. Click the Delete button to proceed or click the Cancel button to do otherwise. A confirmation message will be displayed if the action is successful.
