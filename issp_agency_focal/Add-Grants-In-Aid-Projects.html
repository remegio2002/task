How to Add Grants-In-Aid (GIA) Projects

1. To add Grants-In-Aid (GIA) Projects, click the Add New button (See Figure 5-35). Fill-up the entry boxes that constitute the parameters for adding new Grants-In-Aid (GIA) Projects (See Figure 5-36).


Figure 5-36 Grants-In-Aid (GIA) Projects Scheme Form


2. Type in the Name / Title of ICT Project on the entry box provided.

3. Select the From and To for the Duration on the datepicker.

4. Type in the Objectives and Deliverables on the entry boxes provided.

5. When done, click the Save button to add new Grants-In-Aid (GIA) Projects or the Cancel button to close the form.
