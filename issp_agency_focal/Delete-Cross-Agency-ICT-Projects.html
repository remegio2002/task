How to Delete Cross Agency ICT Projects

1. To delete Cross Agency ICT Projects, click the Table Row as shown in Figure 5-40. Click the Delete button (See Figure 5-43).


Figure 5-43 Delete Cross Agency ICT Projects


2. Once you click the Delete button, delete dialog box will pop-up (See Figure 5-44).


Figure 5-44 Delete Dialog Box


3. Click the Delete button to proceed or click the Cancel button to do otherwise. A confirmation message will be displayed if the action is successful.
