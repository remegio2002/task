How to Delete General Appropriations Act (GAA) Projects

1. To delete General Appropriations Act (GAA) Projects, click the Table Row as shown in Figure 5-45. Click the Delete button (See Figure 5-48).


Figure 5-48 Delete General Appropriations Act (GAA) Projects


2. Once you click the Delete button, delete dialog box will pop-up (See Figure 5-49).


Figure 5-49 Delete Dialog Box


3. Click the Delete button to proceed or click the Cancel button to do otherwise. A confirmation message will be displayed if the action is successful.
