The DOST Technology Hub (TecHub) provides a curated collection of the department's scientific and technological innovations and services. This includes processes on adoption, transfer, and commercialization, It is also a testament to DOST's commitment of bringing science closer to the people. The following DOST priorities are aligned with the President's socioeconomic agenda such as follows: Livelihood, Food, Health, Water & Environment, Energy, Transportation, Climate and Disaster Resiliency, and Digital Transformation.
